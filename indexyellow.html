About Me

A results-oriented Software Engineer with a background in operations and management. I specialize in building intuitive, user-centric web applications where technical precision meets business logic.


My previous career in leadership taught me that the best software isn’t built in a vacuum — it’s the result of clear communication and effective collaboration. I excel in a cross-functional collaboration team with the ability to turn complex requirements into seamless digital experiences. Leveraging my background in client management to ensure every line of code serves the end user and the business goals. I thrive in remote, collaborative environments where my blend of technical expertise and interpersonal strategy can drive a project forward.


I build software with a focus on clarity, usability, and impact. Transitioning from a successful career in operations to frontend development, I bring a unique perspective and "business-first" lens to the engineering process.
